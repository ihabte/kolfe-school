Student Information Chatbot

Connect with School Administration

Our student information chatbot allows parents and students to get quick answers to common questions and connect with school administration when needed.

You can ask about academic schedules, school events, fee payments, and more. For complex issues, the chatbot will connect you directly with the appropriate school staff.

The chatbot is available 24/7, with live support during school hours (8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday).
