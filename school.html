Welcome to Kolfe Excellence Academy

Providing quality education to students in Kolfe, Addis Ababa since 1995. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for a successful future. Kolfe Academy stands as a shining example of educational excellence, embodying all the qualities of an exceptional learning institution. At its core, Kolfe Academy is built upon a foundation of dedicated educators who are not just teachers but mentors passionately committed to nurturing each student's unique potential. The academy creates a safe, inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds feel valued, respected, and empowered to express themselves freely. Beyond academic rigor, Kolfe Academy emphasizes holistic development, fostering character building, emotional intelligence, and creative expression through a rich array of extracurricular activities.

About Our School

Kolfe Excellence Academy is located in the heart of Kolfe, Addis Ababa. Established in 1995, our school has been committed to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from kindergarten to grade 12, with modern facilities and experienced teachers dedicated to student success.

Our vision is to create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning skills.
